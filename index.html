About Us

SENTENG Fashions began its journey in 2019 as a dedicated sewing manufacturer, founded by experienced entrepreneurs with a vision to set new trends in the apparel industry. From the very beginning, we recognized that speed and reliability play a vital role in modern fashion, and that inspired us to establish our base in the heart of Nairobi City Center, along Mfangano Street.

Over the years, SENTENG has grown into one of Kenya’s leading textile manufacturers, equipped with state-of-the-art machinery, advanced printing technology, and a strong trading network to guarantee the highest product quality and safety standards. Our commitment to excellence has earned us recognition, including awards for producing the best quality in T-shirt manufacturing.


We pride ourselves on being a customer-focused and solution-driven organization, fully compliant with client standards and dedicated to delivering beyond expectations.

Today, after years of steady progression, SENTENG Fashions stands as a pioneer in T-shirt manufacturing in Nairobi and a trusted choice for wholesalers and retailers alike. We continue to evolve with the market, driven by innovation, quality, and a passion for fashion.
